    Sponsors and Partners

    Panasonic Lumix - Our official camera sponsor. Please go to their site and check out their line of cameras.
    Epson - Our official printer sponsor. Please go to their site and check out their line of printers.
    WWF - For 50 years, WWF has been protecting the future of nature. The world’s leading conservation organization, WWF works in 100 countries and is supported by more than one million members in the United States and close to five million globally. WWF's unique way of working combines global reach with a foundation in science, involves action at every level from local to global, and ensures the delivery of innovative solutions that meet the needs of both people and nature.
    The community of professional photographers 35PHOTO holds second annual competition 35AWARDS, which has set itself a very ambitious goal: to select the best 100 photos in 2016 with hundreds of thousands of works submitted to the contest. The evaluation system photographs spectators, photographers, passed on 35photo.ru Send moderation, and a professional jury will select the most deserving, fresh, artistic and original photos. Winners receive electronic certificates, and the best works fall into the album, published the results of the contest.
    The Kuala Lumpur Photography Festival (KLPF) is the hottest and biggest imaging event in Malaysia and possibly Southeast Asia. As travel and photography always go hand-in-hand, the KLPF also include activities in the travel section. Each year photographers and travellers converge at the KLPF to attend the seminars from industry leaders, interact with experts, build new relationships, experience new products and win attractive prizes.
    Now in our 8th year, the Kuala Lumpur International Photoawards continues to engage the global photographic community. KLPA remains committed to promoting international contemporary portrait photography from South East Asia and the rest of the world, by rewarding some of the top photographers in the portraiture genre. As in previous years, we seek fresh and innovative photography from new, emerging and established photographers from around the world.
    Better Photography has been the leading photography magazine in India for 19 years now. With a primary audience that includes serious enthusiasts and amateurs, the magazine covers a wide variety of content from techniques and equipment reviews and interviews. BP, as it is often affectionately called, is a hobbyist magazine whose priority is education in the art and science of photography.
    Nature Wanderers is India’s pioneering photography tours and travel company that imparts eld photography training and guidance to amateur and professional photographers across the globe. With over 10 years of experience and 400+ tours under our belt, we conduct wildlife photography tours across national parks of India and also work with photographers for their customised programs with expert photography guides.
